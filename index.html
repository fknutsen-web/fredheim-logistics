How we work

The model is the difference.

Anyone can fix a vessel. What sets Pelorus apart is whose side we're on — and how we're paid.

01

Embedded & exclusive

Your standing freight desk, not a voyage-by-voyage vendor. We work as your commercial department would — and stay exclusive to you within your lanes.

02

A fixed per-ton fee

You pay a set fee per freight ton — never a percentage of the freight bill. We earn the same whether the rate runs high or low, so our only incentive is to drive your freight down.

03

Never a position against you

We don't trade freight or take the other side of your cargo. No spread, no hidden principal — pure representation of the cargo owner, start to finish.

Where we sit

The aligned middle.

A broker's incentive points the wrong way. A trading shop can hold a position against you. An in-house desk is slow and expensive. Pelorus is the option none of them can copy without breaking their own model.

A broker

Finds tonnage, quotes rates.

Paid a commission that rises with your freight bill. Useful — but owns none of your outcome, and the incentive points the wrong way.

A trading shop

May take the other side.

Charters and trades freight as principal. Capable on execution, but can hold a position against your cargo — and is hired voyage by voyage.

An in-house desk

Full control, high fixed cost.

A dedicated team gives control, but is expensive to build, slow to stand up, and hard to staff with senior commercial experience.

Pelorus

Your desk. Your side. Fixed fee.

Embedded senior oversight across the cycle, on a fixed per-ton fee, never a position against you.
